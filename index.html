Flexible Deal Structures

Three options to fit your budget and growth goals.

Option A

Tiered Volume Rebate

Best for: Volume-focused carriers

Pay 2–5% of cardholder shipping spend on your network. Rate increases as volume grows. Monthly reconciliation with full label-level reporting. No upfront cost — pay only on completed shipments.

Option B

Co-Marketing Budget

Best for: Brand-focused carriers

Annual marketing commitment ($500K–$5M). Your logo on the card + welcome kit. Dedicated launch campaign. Co-branded email to our activated $100M shipping base.

Option C

Rate Discount Pass-Through

Best for: Tight-margin carriers

Offer cardholders 15–30% below published rates. No cash rebate required — the discount is the reward. Strongest customer acquisition tool in the product.

All structures are negotiable. We recommend starting with Option A and adding co-marketing in Year 2.
